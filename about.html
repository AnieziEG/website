Our Story

Aniezi Construction Group started as a sole propriator business in the field of electrical engineering in Okposi Ohaozara Local Area of Ebonyi state.
In present time the company has grown to have branches in major cities of Nigeria and outside Nigeria, some of the countries include the following and we still accept partnership from all over the world.

  • Head Office in Nigeria
  • Representative in Cameron
  • Representative in Benin Replubic

We are dedicated to developing Africa and making the world see what Africans are made of, by promoting and encouraging young African engineers to global level. So you can write to us to partner with us from any part of the world as we are ever ready to do business with you.
To reach out to us go through our contact page, also check our youtube video for more.
